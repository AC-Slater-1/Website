Development Roadmap

APPBUSTER

"Same tools. Same features. $0/mo."

Est. 2026 · Built with AI · Free forever

The Problem

Small and mid-size businesses spend $5,000 to $50,000 per year on SaaS subscriptions they can barely afford. Semrush charges $249/mo for SEO tools. HubSpot charges $800/mo for a CRM. Monday.com charges $79/mo for a task board. Most businesses use 10% of the features and pay 100% of the price.

The Insight

AI can now build functional software in hours, not months. The cost to develop and maintain a CRM, an email tool, or an SEO suite has collapsed by 90%+. But SaaS companies haven't passed those savings to customers. We will.

The Model

Think store brands, but for software. Same functionality as name-brand SaaS, built from scratch with AI, given away for free.

  • Every app is free. No freemium tricks, no 14-day trials, no credit card required.
  • AI-built from scratch. Not cloned, not pirated, not forked. Original code, built with AI.
  • "Compare to" positioning. Just like Kirkland vs. Tide. Our NexRank vs. their Semrush.
  • Revenue from premium support, custom deployments, and enterprise features.

The Market

The global SaaS market is $197B and growing 12% YoY. There are 33 million small businesses in the US alone. Most are simply paying for software they don't fully use. We're not trying to beat Salesforce. We're trying to save the businesses that can't afford Salesforce.
